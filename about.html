About Me

Hello, I'm Ben.

I'm a final-year Management Information Systems student at Texas A&M University, on track to graduate in May 2027. My academic focus sits at the intersection of technology and business — I'm drawn to how information systems can drive smarter decisions, streamline operations, and create lasting value for organizations.

My interests span technology consulting, business analysis, product management, project management, and network engineering. I enjoy working on problems that require both a technical mindset and strategic thinking — whether that means mapping out a business process, scoping a product, or figuring out how systems talk to each other. I thrive in collaborative environments and love working with people who take their craft seriously.

Outside of school, I'm a big tech enthusiast — I love building custom PCs, gaming when I get the chance, and staying current on everything happening in the tech world. I'm a college football fan and enjoy staying active, whether that's working out or playing sports with friends. I love to travel; some of my favorite places I've visited are Hawaii, Ireland, and Yosemite National Park. I also have a Chocolate Lab named Charlie Brown who keeps life interesting. I'm originally from the Austin area and currently based in College Station finishing my degree.
